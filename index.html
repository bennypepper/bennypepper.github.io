Academic Research

Evaluasi Efektivitas Indikator CBBI untuk Klasifikasi Return Bitcoin

Ongoing PKL Research

Keywords: Quantitative Finance, Machine Learning, Backtesting, On-chain Metrics

An empirical evaluation of the Colin Talks Crypto Bitcoin Bull Run Index (CBBI) as a classification signal for market cycles. This research utilizes Backtesting and Machine Learning (Random Forest & Logistic Regression) on historical data spanning 3 halving cycles to identify the most predictive on-chain indicators for forward returns.

FitMe: Inovasi Alat Pemindai Label Obat Herbal Berbahasa Mandarin

Ongoing PKM-KI Research

Keywords: Health-Tech, Rule-Based Chatbot, Python, WhatsApp API, Patient Safety

An interdisciplinary research and development project funded under the national Student Creativity Program (PKM-KI). Focusing on a digital solution that integrates a web platform and a Python chatbot to scan and translate Mandarin herbal medicine labels. The system educates users on safe dosages and potential drug interactions to prevent unsupervised self-medication and kidney damage.

Perbandingan Arsitektur CNN untuk Klasifikasi Pneumonia

Academic Manuscript

Keywords: Deep Learning, EfficientNet, Transfer Learning, Progressive Resizing

A comparative study on training strategies for classifying Bacterial vs. Viral Pneumonia using Pediatric Chest X-Rays. The research demonstrated that fixed-size resolution training outperformed progressive resizing, achieving a final accuracy of 87.14% and an ROC AUC of 0.9431, optimizing computer-aided diagnostic reliability.
